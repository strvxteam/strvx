# meetings/

One page per scheduled meeting (`public.bookings`). Meeting prep briefs (`public.meeting_prep_briefs`) fold into the page's frontmatter as `prep_brief`.

## Does NOT go here

- Email threads — they're conversations, not meetings. → folded into person/company timelines.
- Internal team standups without external attendees (no signal to capture).

## Slug rule

`YYYY-MM-DD-<short-title>`. The date stays stable; the title is the booking's `service_type` or notes summary.
