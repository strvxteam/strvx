# companies/

One page per organization. Includes both client-side companies (`public.companies`) and channel partners (`public.partners`).

`type: company` in frontmatter distinguishes a client (or prospect) from a partner.

## Does NOT go here

- The deal itself — that goes in `deals/`. A company page links to its deal(s).
- Specific projects shipping for that company — those go in `projects/`.

## Slug rule

Short canonical name, lowercased, hyphenated. E.g., `acme` not `acme-corp-inc`. Use the company's stable shortname, not the legal entity name.
